\abstract{%
This is a placeholder abstract for the PRLab Tech Report Template, intended to illustrate how the framed abstract block renders on the first page of a PRLab @ NJU tech report or arXiv preprint, including its purple border, sans-serif heading, and the metadata rows that appear immediately below (date, code, project page, correspondence). Replace this paragraph with your own abstract: a concise statement of the problem you study, the central idea or method you propose, the experimental setting in which it is evaluated, and the principal findings or contributions. A good tech-report abstract is typically 150 to 250 words, is self-contained without forward references to later sections, and avoids notation that has not yet been introduced. The remaining sections --- Introduction, Method, Experiments, Conclusion, and Appendix --- are stubs under \texttt{sec/} that you can edit freely, and the bibliography lives in \texttt{references.bib}.
}
